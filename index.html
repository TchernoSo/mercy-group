Our Mission

Dear brothers, one of the most difficult moments in life is when a family member passes away. The family of the deceased needs support, both emotionally and financially, especially in Western countries, where burial costs are very high.

Burial costs can exceed £5,000, and these costs are rising. Therefore, we must come together and cooperate by contributing towards these expenses. Our system is simple: each member contributes £100 initially. Whenever the fund decreases, each member contributes the same amount again. There are no monthly or yearly subscriptions—just support when needed.

We believe this is one of the best ways of cooperation in righteousness and piety, as Allah Almighty has commanded. We invite all brothers and sisters to join this noble cause.
